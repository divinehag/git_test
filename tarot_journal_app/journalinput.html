Tarot Journal Generator: Body/Mind/Spirit Spread

This tool allows you to generate a simple blog/journal entry by inputting information from a Body/Mind/Spirit (or any three-card) tarot spread. It will automatically generate the time and date of the reading, so be sure to edit before posting if your entering information from an older spread!

Enter your information, then click "Generate." The entry will appear on the right side of the page.

The format for this spread and reading comes from Mary K. Greer's Tarot for Your Self. If you'd like to learn more about tarot, here are some great online resources that I found helpful:

  • Biddy Tarot
  • Kawaii Tarot
  • Little Red Tarot Blog (archived as of Oct. 2018)
